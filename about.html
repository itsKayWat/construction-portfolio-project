About BuildRight Construction

Our Story

Building Excellence for Over 18 Years

BuildRight Construction was founded in 2005 by Michael Johnson, a third-generation builder with a vision to create a construction company that prioritizes quality, craftsmanship, and client satisfaction above all else.

What started as a small residential renovation company has grown into a full-service construction firm with a reputation for excellence in both residential and commercial projects. Today, BuildRight Construction employs a team of skilled professionals who share our commitment to superior construction practices and exceptional customer service.

We're proud to have completed hundreds of successful projects throughout the region, from custom homes and major renovations to commercial buildings and specialized construction. Our growth and success come from our unwavering dedication to quality and our ability to forge lasting relationships with our clients.
